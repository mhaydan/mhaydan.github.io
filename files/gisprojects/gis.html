Mobility Index Analysis, Auckland


Aiming towards sustainability, the world is now shifting to a pedestrian and cycling-based spatial development. With the new trend of work-from home culture, it is very important to have the basic amenities in walking distance such as retail, gym, or leisure in a residential environment. In the mobility index, the study was carried out to analyze the spatial development and behavior of the pedestrians in Auckland. The index based on the facilities in the range of 1.5 km from each household. In the process of mapping walkability index, four weighted factors considered to form the equation: Transport Index x1.5 + Accessibility Index x1.3 + Safety Index x1.3 + Quality Index x1
